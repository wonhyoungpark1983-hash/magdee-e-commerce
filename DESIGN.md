## üß© Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: Black background, white text, hover lift effect
- **Secondary Button**: Outlined style, fills on hover
- **Accent Button**: Gold tone background

### Cards
- **Product Card**: White background, rounded corners, hover shadow and lift effect
- **Image**: Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- **Brand**: Uppercase, small text, gray color
- **Product Name**: Medium weight, dark text
- **Price**: Bold, large text

### Input Fields
- Clean borders with focus states
- Error states in red
- Icon support on the left

### Navigation Bar
- Sticky header with backdrop blur
- Minimalist design with logo, menu items, and icons
- Mobile hamburger menu
